I started cooking because I love to eat and it was the most affordable way to have good meals. I keep cooking now because I love being able to experiment with new ways of cooking new ingredients or new recipes. The process of smelling and tasting while I cook makes the whole thing enjoyable, and when other people take part in it, it makes it all the better. There are still certain tastes that I just can't get in a restaurant, like a recipe I grew up with, that still makes cooking an essential part of my life. My favorite items to cook are Indian dishes like Chicken Biriyani, Tandoori Chicken, etc.

I love listening to songs because some express my thoughts, feelings, and emotions through strong lyrics and music. I also feel that music can express happiness, sadness, or even anxiety in any situation. Many days, I just listen to good songs and relax. In addition to providing relaxation therapy, songs also let me go back to some good times of my life and have a positive mindset in challenging times of life. My favorite songs are Tamil Songs composed by Illayaraja and AR Rahman.
